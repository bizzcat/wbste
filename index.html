My training

For the last six years, I’ve been full-time devoted to training in spiritual, therapeutic, and relational practices.

Four of those years were spent living in multiple Buddhist monasteries — a year and a half of which on silent meditation retreat, under the steady guidance of skilled teachers.

Another central practice has been Circling, which cultivates trust and intimacy in every relationship, helps one navigate charged moments with steadiness, and invites a way of living that is more truthful, courageous, and natural.

I’ve received more than I can ever recount from teachers, monasteries, friends, foundations, and a loving and loyal family along this journey. This coaching practice is one way of paying that forward: sharing everything I’ve learned with anyone who feels they might benefit.

The offering

I’m here to help you:

Develop a deep trust in life and hone the ability to turn every aspect of it — no matter how challenging — into spiritual gold, into fuel for your own self-understanding.

Temper your inner-critic and tell more honest and compassionate stories about yourself and others.

Have fun, let loose, and give full expression to your creativity and quirky eccentricities — all while staying grounded, disciplined, and showing up for responsibilities.

Re-integrate parts of you driven into exile, mediate between the parts of you at odds with each other, and realize your innate wholeness.

Learn how to advocate for your emotional needs, improve family relations, set and maintain boundaries, repair strained relationships, release to respectfully say ‘No’ to anything you are not a ‘Hell Yes’ to, advocate for your emotional needs in a relationship, let go of relationships

Cultivate qualities of heart and mind that yield a richer experience of life: gratitude, forgiveness, compassion, focus,

Two Disclaimers:

A common orientation to self-development is to ‘change who we are’ in ways that are violent to the self. That’s not what this is about … you are not broken, and I'm not here to fix you.

I’m still learning each of these for myself — some more developed than others — and will thus be imperfect in my guidance. Consider me your clumsy comrade on the path, not your infallible guru. Nor am I a therapist, so if specialized trauma treatment is what you’re seeking, I'm not your guy.

Session format

One hour of exploring what’s most alive for you, together. Within that hour, either you set the agenda, we figure it out together, or I take the lead based on what I sense would serve.

We might curate a meditation curriculum based on your goals, with regular check-ins and guided meditations to keep momentum.

I can witness you without judgment as you navigate difficult emotions and speak to life’s challenges, offering gentle reflections, reframes, nudges, and — only rarely and with consent — advice.

I’ve discovered that play is often half the medicine. So if what’s needed is to bop around our rooms and babble like banshees, or launch into a howling recital of Bohemian Rhapsody … I’m here for it.

We can explore such themes as:

embodiment, boundaries, control vs. surrender, masculine–feminine dynamics, making wise commitments, giving and receiving feedback skillfully, reclaiming anger and wielding it cleanly, victimhood & self-responsibility, how to navigate manipulative relationships, nervous system regulation, and using the breath to cultivate the mind

Donation-based pricing

Generosity at the center — not as an afterthought.

While I consider myself supremely lucky, I know it’s not just luck that allowed me to train as deeply as I have. Sure — ingenuity, effort, and unearned privilege were a part of it, but the single greatest enabling factor has been generosity.

While the market suggests I could charge a few hundred dollars per session, I’m offering them instead by donation to anyone who feels they might benefit.

This means that after our session you could send me a single dollar and I would rejoice. Truly — you could send me $500 and I wouldn’t hold a single grudge against you.

Whatever you offer enables me to live a simple life and:

∿ Freely coach others

∿ Write with more regularity

∿ Support altruistic projects

∿ Show up for my troubled country

∿ And dance on the weekend with friends

If donation-based is uncomfortable, we can talk through that — sometimes money brings up a lot of material, and that’s welcome here.

Or we can simply set a fixed fee together that feels clear and workable for both of us.

My hope is that this structure allows people with less access to resources to still receive support, while those with more can help sustain the work for others.

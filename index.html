The offering

I’m here to help you:

Develop a deep trust in life and hone the ability to turn every aspect of it — no matter how challenging — into fuel for your own self-understanding.

Remedy your inner-critic and tell more honest and compassionate stories about yourself and others.

Clarify your big Why for being alive, and bring your actions, circumstances, and commitments into alignment with that.

Have fun, let loose, and give full expression to your creativity and quirky eccentricities — all while staying grounded and showing up responsibly for the rest of life.

Re-integrate parts of you tucked away out of fear, shame, or insecurity, mediate between the parts of you at odds with each other, and evermore deeply realize your innate wholeness.

Maintain a regulated nervous system, stay physically fit, cultivate the mind via the breath, and develop a deeper relationship with your body, intuition, and emotions.

Tend to each of your relationships [family, friends, coworkers, partners], foster healthier communication, give and receive feedback skillfully, reclaim disowned anger and wield it cleanly, set and maintain boundaries, and resolve obstacles to intimacy.

Recognize the many forms of manipulation, navigate out of relationships where it is strongly present, clear out the unhealthy psychological residue from the relationship, find forgiveness, forgo victimhood, and move onward with an even deeper self-trust and sovereignty.

A common orientation to self-development is to ‘change who I am’ in ways that are subtly violent to the self. That’s not what this is about … you are not broken, and I'm not here to fix you. Deep wisdom lies within you — let's discover it together.

Session format

One hour of my undivided attention.

Within that hour, either you set the agenda, we figure it out together, or I take the lead based on what I sense would serve.

We might curate a meditation curriculum based on your goals, with regular check-ins and guided meditations to keep momentum.

You can navigate difficult emotions and speak to life’s challenges while I offer gentle reflections, reframes, nudges, and — only rarely and with consent — advice.

I’ve discovered that play is often half the medicine, so if what’s needed is to bop around our rooms and babble like banshees, or launch into a howling recital of Bohemian Rhapsody … I’m here for it.

My training

For the last six years, I’ve been full-time devoted to training in spiritual, therapeutic, and relational practices.

Four of those years were spent living in multiple Buddhist monasteries — a year and a half of which on silent meditation retreat, under the steady guidance of skilled teachers.

Another central practice has been Circling, which cultivates trust and intimacy in relationships, helps one navigate charged moments with steadiness, and invites a way of living that is more truthful, courageous, and natural.

I’ve received more than I can ever recount from teachers, monasteries, friends, foundations, and a loving and loyal family along this journey. This coaching practice is one way of paying that forward: sharing everything I’ve learned with anyone who feels they might benefit, with a focus on affordability.

Donation-based pricing

Generosity at the center — not as an afterthought.

While I consider myself supremely lucky, I know it’s not just luck that allowed me to train as deeply as I have. Sure — ingenuity, effort, and unearned privilege were a part of it, but the single greatest enabling factor has been generosity.

While the market suggests I could charge a few hundred dollars per session, I’m offering them instead by donation to anyone who feels they might benefit.

This means that after our session you could send me a single dollar and I would rejoice. Truly — you could send me $500 and I wouldn’t hold a single grudge against you.

Whatever you offer enables me to live a simple life and:

∿ Write with more regularity
